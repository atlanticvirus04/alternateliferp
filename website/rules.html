Rules and Regulations

Here at Alternate Life, we take our rules seriously. Here is a full list of them.

  • Homepage

General Rules

1.1 Microphone - You must have a working microphone to join the server. Failiure to do so will result in being kicked until you can prove you have a working microphone

1.2 RDM - Random Death Match, you must initiate or have a valid reason to kill or attack another player.

1.3 VDM - Vehicle Death Match, your vehicle may not be used as a weapon unless your life is put in immediate danger.

1.4 FailRP - Fail Role play, is carrying out something unrealistic or inhuman. Do not do things you would not do in real life.

1.5 FearRP - You should always value your life, the only time you may contest this rule if its a 1v1 situation.

1.6 Initiation - Initiation cannot be done through a vehicle. You have to be outside a vehicle to initiate.

1.7 Meta Gaming - Meta gaming is using information outside of the game to help with your role play, Do not use discord/OOC to gather information to be used in game.

1.8 Power Gaming - Power Gaming involves using non game mechanics to your benefit, e.g. role playing you have a explosive when you do not.

1.9 Breaking RP - Breaking RP falls under Fail RP, you should never break character. Always wait until the RP situation finishes before going OOC.

2.0 Combat Logging - You should never log out during an RP situation.

2.1 Exploitation - Abusing any bugs/exploits in game will result in an immediate ban.

2.2 Refunds - In game refunds due to glitches/un expected restarts will only be handed out with proof.

2.3 Refunds - Accidently selling vehicles/houses/boats will NOT be refunded.

2.4 Corruption - Transfering items from NHS/MET to regular characters/players is strictly prohibited, if caught both parties will receive a permanent ban.

2.5 Jail Exploits - You may not kill anyone in prison to avoid jailtime.

Character Rules

3.0 NLR - New Life Rule, when you die you forget about your previous life and are not aloud to continue any RP. This does not include being revived by a Medkit/NHS

3.1 Terrorism - Role playing any sort of terrorism will result in an immediate ban.

3.2 Sexual RP - Sexual role play/assault of any kind will result in an immediate ban.

3.3 Impersonation - Impersonation of MET/NHS will result in an immediate ban.

3.4 Death Avoidance - You cannot pass money or weapons as youâ€™re about to die to avoid the death system.

3.5 Restraints - If you are restrained you are unable to communicate with any parties apart from regular voice chat.

3.6 Hostages - You may not take MORE than 2 people hostage in any scenario. The negotiator CANNOT be taken hostage. MAXIMUM 2 MET Police Officers Hostage at once, Must ALWAYS have 1 more MET Officer on duty to negotiate.

Faction Rules

4.0 Group Sizes - No larger groups than 5 are allowed to role play in one given scenario.

4.1 NHS - NHS are a neutral faction they may not be harmed.

4.2 NHS/MET - The use of MET/NHS vehicles when not playing the correct faction will result in an immediate ban.

4.3 Cop Baiting - Cop baiting or trolling officers is not allowed

4.4 NHS - When revived by NHS you are in their custody until you are released and deemed safe.

4.5 Robberies - There has to be at least 30 minutes space between every robbery.

4.6 Robberies - During bank/store robberies ALL of the attackers have to be inside you are not allowed to place snipers or outsiders.

4.7 Gangs - Maximum 5 players committing a gang crime at the same time.

4.8 Sirens - Sirens do not count as initiation, you may not fire upon Police if they are chasing you until you initiate directly with the police.

4.9 Bounty Hunter/Assasin - No role playing as any of these roles, killing someone for a bounty is against the rules. Capturing and hostage taking for bounties are allowed.

5.0 Vehicle Violence - Shooting/Initiation out of vehicles is strictly prohibited.
